Our Menu

Tajine

A slow-cooked stew braised at low temperatures, traditionally cooked in a clay or ceramic pot.

Couscous

A traditional Berber dish of steamed semolina served with meat or vegetables.

Pastilla

A savory-sweet pie traditionally made with squab (fledgling pigeon), today often made with chicken or seafood.

Harira

A traditional Moroccan soup made with tomatoes, lentils, chickpeas, and a mix of spices.

Zaalouk

A delicious cooked salad made from eggplants, tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, and spices.

Briouats

Small Moroccan pastries filled with meat or seafood, herbs, and spices, wrapped in warqa pastry and fried.

Kefta

Ground meat mixed with spices, often served as meatballs or on skewers.

Rfissa

A festive dish of chicken, lentils, and msemen (a type of Moroccan pancake), spiced with fenugreek and ras el hanout.

Msemen

A traditional Moroccan flatbread, often served with honey or stuffed with savory fillings.
